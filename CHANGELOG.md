# Changelog

All notable changes to R7's Online Guide are recorded here, newest first.
This mirrors the in-page **"What's New"** panel. Dates are the publish month.
Nothing is ever deleted — the history is the point.

---

## v1.8 — Personalized audit checklist — July 2026

- Added a **Personalize your checklist** quiz to Section 4 — a few quick
  questions (kids or elderly dependents in your care, paid monitoring/removal/
  security services, VPN, router admin, social media use, etc.) that filter
  the 29-item audit down to only what applies to you.
- Answers are stored locally on your device only — nothing is sent anywhere.
- Progress counts, category chips, and the overall completion percentage now
  reflect only your personalized item list; categories with no relevant items
  (e.g. Email & Communications) hide themselves automatically.
- The quiz is excluded from the Print / Save as PDF output, so your exported
  checklist stays clean.
- You can reopen the quiz anytime to change your answers, or skip it to see
  the full original checklist.

## UI refresh (no version change) — July 2026

- Refreshed the visual theme: dark mode now uses a leather/steel palette and
  light mode now uses an aluminum/digital-blue palette, echoing the site's
  logo.
- Added collapsible topic sections with an "Expand all" / "Collapse all"
  control, so the guide feels lighter-weight to skim.
- Added Essential / Recommended / Optional priority badges to the Section 1
  topics.
- Renamed "Suggestions" to "Important Strategies" and "Opt-Out" to "Digital
  Privacy Guide" in the section navigation and headings; renamed the Opt-Out
  "Part 1" heading to "Opt-Out Strategies and DIY Guides".
- Fixed a contrast bug where two section-nav tab labels were hardcoded to the
  wrong theme's colors instead of following the active theme.

This is a UI/UX-only update — no guide content, advice, or version number
changed.

## v1.7 — Beginner on-ramp, corrections & open licensing — July 2026

- Added a **Start Here** panel: the five highest-impact steps, with honest
  time estimates, for readers who are new to all of this.
- Added a **Plain-English Glossary** defining every technical term used in the
  guide.
- Added **AnnualCreditReport.com** — the free official source for one credit
  report per bureau — to both the Credit Freeze section and the annual audit.
- Added **Yubico** product and **NYT Wirecutter** guide links to the physical
  security-key advice.
- **Browser-extension corrections:** replaced the link that pointed to
  `ublock.org` (an abandoned fork) with the official **uBlock Origin**; removed
  **HTTPS Everywhere** (retired by the EFF — browsers now do this natively);
  dropped **Adblock Plus** and **Ghostery** in favour of one recommended
  blocker; added a Chrome note (Chrome now only supports the reduced **uBlock
  Origin Lite**).
- **Messaging corrections:** removed **BitChat** (experimental, no independent
  security audit, fake copies circulating) and moved **Telegram** to the
  caution list — its regular chats are *not* end-to-end encrypted by default.
- Made the **Complete Site Directory** toggle far more noticeable, and fixed
  the Section 4 *Print / Save as PDF* button overlapping the section heading.
- Replaced the vague footer notice with an explicit **CC BY-NC-SA 4.0** license
  and a transparency note about how the guide is maintained.

## v1.6 — Annual Audit & navigation upgrades — July 2026

- Restructured Section 3 into Part 1 (Opt-Out Services & DIY Guides, with a
  step-by-step hybrid strategy), Part 2 (Data Brokers), and Part 3 (People
  Search Sites).
- Section 4 audit gained category jump-chips, a "last completed" date, a
  quarterly-cadence reminder for opt-outs, an antivirus performance-bloat
  check, a 100% completion banner, and a *Save my audit as PDF* button.
- Added the *What's New* tracker and "New" markers for freshly added content.

## v1.5 — Complete opt-out directory — June 2026

- Added the **Complete Site Directory** in Section 3 — 190+ people-search,
  phone, background-check, property, and genealogy sites, categorized with a
  live filter.
- Added a **Priority Hubs** shortlist and a *Save this list as PDF* export.
- Verified and filled in direct opt-out links for the majority of listed sites.

## v1.4 and earlier — Foundation
